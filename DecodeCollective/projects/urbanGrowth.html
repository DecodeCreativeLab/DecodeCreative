Potentials Of Geo-spatial Expansion

Urban Growth

This project is considered as an exercise trying to explore new ways of predicting future evolution of the urban tissue. By gathering data indicating any sort of activity, the estmated areas of urban development are established. The studied data layers derive from various sources and illustrate potential of movement, presence of many people and latest constructions. These areas constitute the seeds of future urban regeneration. Therefore, cellular automata are used as a generative algorithm that by changing the rules of growth according to spatial characteristics, a potential estimation of how the city might look like in the future is investigated. These structures are a diagramatic representation of density of activity. In other words, they could represent the bounding box for building volumes. As, soon as the generation is completed the resulted configuration is excavated in areas where public amenities are met. However, the overall volume is retained as the excavated parts are regenerated in other available positions. The final result depicts an estimation of one of many version of urban development.
